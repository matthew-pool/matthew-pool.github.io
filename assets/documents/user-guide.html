What is Flick?

Flick is a vertical swiping video app designed for optimized, seamless video discovery. It provides a continuous stream of videos from the vast library of archive.org. Flick intermittently feeds the user the most likely video to be enjoyed by that specific user, using metadata weights based on the userâ€™s video likes, dislikes, and view times, all of which are uploaded to a backend cloud environment for analysis of the anonymized data.

Leaving a Sideways Chain

When you're done exploring a sideways chain, you have two options:

  • Swipe Down This destroys the sideways chain and takes you back to the exact video on the main feed where you first swiped left.
  • Swipe Up This also destroys the chain but advances you to the next video in the main feed, right after the one that started the chain.
