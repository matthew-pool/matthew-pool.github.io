# Privacy Policy for Flick

**Last Updated:** November 10, 2025

## Introduction

Flick ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android video application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## Information We Collect

### Anonymous Usage Data

Flick collects limited, anonymous usage information to improve our service:

- **Video ID**: Identifier of videos you watch
- **View Time**: Duration of time spent watching videos
- **Interactions**: Like and dislike actions on videos

This data is collected **anonymously** and is not linked to your personal identity, device ID, or any other identifying information.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, advertising ID)
- Location data
- Contact lists or other personal data from your device

## How We Use Your Information

The anonymous data we collect is used solely to:
- Understand video engagement and popularity
- Improve content recommendations
- Enhance overall app performance and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- All collected data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
- Data is transmitted over secure, encrypted connections
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. The anonymous usage data is used exclusively for internal analytics purposes.

## Children's Privacy

Flick does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us.

## Your Rights

Since we only collect anonymous data that cannot be linked back to you:
- There is no personal data to access, modify, or delete
- You can stop data collection at any time by uninstalling the app

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Third-Party Services

Flick uses Firebase, a service provided by Google LLC. Firebase may collect additional anonymous analytics data. Please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more information.
